La Ronde is Eastern Canada's largest amusement park. The facility, which opened as part of Expo '67, today offers some 40 rides and attractions, includ ing the Goliath, one of North America's highest and fastest roller coasters, and Le Pays de Ribambelle, an exciting and enchanting family area.
